Goal/s

The goals for the website are to ensure that people are more knowledgeable about the CoffeeCan business. We want to ensure that people can have one simple place where they can access menus, prices, specials and other various information like cart hiring. The button placed on the home page ensures that a user can quickly and easily access the menu. Whilst the navbar clearly shows where to contact and learn more about the business A goal of the website is to ensure a user can communicate without hassle with the business. With multiple links to the CoffeeCan's social media pages and a form that the user can fill out, we hope that customers can get in touch.
